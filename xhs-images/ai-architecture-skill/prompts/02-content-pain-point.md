Create a Xiaohongshu (Little Red Book) style infographic following these guidelines:

## Image Specifications

- **Type**: Infographic
- **Orientation**: Portrait (vertical)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 3:4
- **Style**: Hand-drawn illustration

## Core Principles

- Hand-drawn quality throughout - NO realistic or photographic elements
- Keep information concise, highlight keywords and core concepts
- Use ample whitespace for easy visual scanning
- Maintain clear visual hierarchy

## Text Style (CRITICAL)

- **ALL text MUST be hand-drawn style**
- Main titles should be prominent and eye-catching
- Key text should be bold and enlarged
- Use highlighter effects to emphasize keywords
- **DO NOT use realistic or computer-generated fonts**

## Language

- Use Chinese (简体中文)

---

## Style: Warm

**Color Palette**:
- Primary: Warm orange (#ED8936), golden yellow (#F6AD55), terracotta (#C05621)
- Background: Cream (#FFFAF0), soft peach (#FED7AA)
- Accents: Deep brown (#744210), soft red (#E57373)

**Visual Elements**:
- Warm lighting effects, friendly decorations
- Soft comfortable shapes, paper texture background

**Typography**:
- Friendly rounded hand lettering, warm color accents

---

## Layout: Balanced

**Information Density**: Medium (3-4 key points)
**Whitespace**: 35-45% of canvas

---

## Content

**Position**: Content (Page 2 of 6)
**Core Message**: 朋友做跨境电商的真实故事

**Text Content**:
- Title: 「朋友的翻车故事」
- Point 1: AI一天生成200条listing
- Point 2: 三个月后，转化率反而降了
- Point 3: 200条长得一模一样：标题、卖点、语气全一样
- Bottom: 他缺的不是AI，是架构

**Visual Concept**:
左边一摞相似的文档堆在一起（用手绘风格），右边一个下降的趋势箭头。中间一个卡通小人挠头困惑。整体cream色背景，温暖手绘风。
