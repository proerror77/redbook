# 配图方案：Coinbase AI 裁员短评

## 配图目标

- 平台：X.com
- 规格：16:9 单图观点卡
- 用途：强化核心判断，不做新闻海报，不做 logo 拼贴
- 语气：冷静、克制、管理判断感强

## 核心命题

> AI 让 ship 更快，不等于组织真的学会了。

## 视觉锚点

- 左侧是高亮的速度表征：更快的交付轨迹、冲刺中的任务流、被压缩的时间感。
- 右侧是被忽略的组织能力：review、权限、责任、回滚、知识沉淀，表现为半透明的管理结构层或检查节点。
- 画面冲突：前景一切都像在加速，背景却出现缺失的结构件，形成“速度上来了，但组织骨架可能没补上”的张力。

## X 观点卡 Prompt

### base broth

Editorial business-tech illustration for an X opinion card, cinematic but restrained, management-systems tension, credible enterprise AI atmosphere, no meme energy, no cartoon mascot, no logo collage, no photoreal faces.

### style seasoning

- palette: steel blue, slate gray, muted white, one controlled warning red accent
- lighting: cool directional light with subtle depth haze
- mood: sober, analytical, slightly unsettling
- rendering: premium digital editorial illustration
- composition: wide 16:9, clear focal hierarchy, room for headline text

### content variables

- subject: a shrinking org chart / team structure being compressed by an AI acceleration arrow
- contrast: visible speed in the foreground, invisible missing governance in the background
- symbolic elements:
  - fast-moving task cards / delivery streaks
  - half-built review gates
  - faded labels suggesting permissions, rollback, knowledge, accountability
- avoid:
  - no Coinbase logo dependency
  - no literal layoff scene
  - no crying workers
  - no stock-photo office
  - no SVG-like flat infographic look

### text budget

- headline max 14 Chinese chars per line
- recommended overlay:
  - line 1: `Ship 更快`
  - line 2: `不等于组织学会了`
- optional corner label:
  - `AI 裁员叙事`
- keep on-image text minimal; let the post carry the full argument

### layout QA

- reserve safe text area in left-center or upper-left
- keep main subject on right half to avoid text collision
- maintain thumbnail readability on X mobile feed
- ensure red accent occupies less than 8% of frame
- do not let overlay text overlap key symbolic elements
- no tiny labels that become noise after feed compression

## 使用说明

- 这是 prompt 草案，不实际生图。
- 如果后续要交给 `/baoyu-image-gen`，直接沿用这份 recipe 结构即可。
- 若需要更强新闻感，可在角落加极小日期或 `AI layoff narrative` 英文标签，但不要让它变成媒体封面风。
